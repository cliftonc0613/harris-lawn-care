Our Story

15 Years of Growing Anderson's Most Beautiful Lawns

What started as a passion for creating beautiful outdoor spaces has grown into Anderson's most trusted lawn care service.

Harris Professional Lawn Care has been serving the Anderson, SC community since 2009. Over the past 15 years, we've built our reputation on reliability, quality work, and treating every lawn as if it were our own.

We understand the unique challenges of maintaining lawns in South Carolina's climate—from the hot, humid summers to the unpredictable spring rains. Our experience means we know exactly what your lawn needs, when it needs it.
